#### DHT22 pinout
![DHT22 pinout](https://i1.wp.com/randomnerdtutorials.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/dht22-pinout.png?w=812&ssl=1)



DHT pin | Connect to 
--------|-----------
1   |   3.3v
2   |   Any digital GPIO; also connect a 10k Ohm pull-up resistor
3   |   Don't connect
4   |   GND
